What, or Who is Doge?


Doge was an Internet meme that became popular in 2013, the meme is a picture of a Shiba Inu breed dog accompanied by multicolored text in Comic Sans font.
The text represents a kind of internal monologue, and is often written in broken English, emphasizing words like "so", "such", "many", "much", and "very".


It's popularity was believed to be influenced by the peculiar look the animal had at the camera--is it happy to see me or is it secretly judging me?
Kabosu, a female Shiba Inu is the dog featured in this meme, was a rescue dog adopted by Atsuko Sato from Japan.
The images originated from the owner's blog sharing various pictures of Kabosu, while this one caught the Internet's attention in particular.


What about Shiba Inu?


Shiba Inu is small-to-medium sized dog. it is one of the smallest of the six original and distinct Spitz breeds native to Japan.
While it is small, it is very agile and muscular that copes well in mountainous terrain, originally bred for hunting animals like Wild Boars.


The term Shiba means brushwood in Japanese, and many drawn similarities of its name to its brushwood color similar to shrubs.
Its frame is compact with well-developed muscles, and is double coated--the outer coat being stiff and straight, while the undercoat is soft and thick.
They also have fox-like face, ears, and legs, making people often confuse them with foxes.
